We value your feedback and are here to assist you with any questions, concerns, or support you may need. Please feel free to reach out to us.

This page is accessible on deployed site `https://memorizii.vercel.app/contact` or locally on `http://localhost:3000/contact`

## Contact Informations

To contact us, please fill out the form below by following these steps:

1. Enter your first name.
2. Enter your last name.
3. Enter your email address.
4. Enter your subject.
5. Enter your message.
6. Click the <span className="text-orange-400">Let's Talk</span> button.
